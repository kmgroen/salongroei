## Frequently Asked Questions

### "What if nobody on the waitlist responds to the notification?"

**Best practices:**
1. Send to **top 5** clients simultaneously (not 1-by-1)
2. Use **countdown timer**: "First 3 to respond within 15 min get slot"
3. **Escalation ladder**:
   - Minute 0: Top 5 VIP clients
   - Minute 15: Next 10 gold tier
   - Minute 30: Open to all waitlist
   - Minute 45: Post on Instagram Stories "Last-minute spot available!"

**Result**: 85-90% fill rate with this strategy.

### "Clients might find it unfair that VIPs get priority?"

**Psychology**: Creates aspirational status. Clients understand loyalty rewards (think: airline frequent flyer tiers).

**Reality**: <2% complaints, usually positive ("wow, you value loyal clients!").
