## 5 Unexpected Benefits of 24/7 Booking

### 1. The "Impulse Booking" (21:00-24:00)
15% of online bookings happen between 9 PM-midnight. These are often:
- Last-minute decisions after social media
- Gift bookings for partners/friends
- Party/event preparations

**This group has 35% higher average ticket value** (extra services, products).

### 2. Less "Phone Tag" Frustration
"I've been trying to call for 3 days but can't get through!"

With online booking:
- Client books directly when convenient
- No frustration over busy lines
- Better client experience = higher reviews

### 3. Automatic Upselling Opportunities
During the online booking process:
- "Want to add a keratin treatment directly? (Save €15)"
- "Book your next appointment now and receive 10% discount"

**Result**: 23% of online bookings add extra services vs. 8% for phone bookings.

### 4. Data & Pattern Recognition
With online booking you see:
- Which services are most booked
- Most popular time slots
- Seasonal patterns
- New vs. returning clients

**Use this for**: Smarter staff planning, targeted marketing, optimal pricing.

### 5. Lower Barrier for New Clients
New clients find calling intimidating:
- "Which stylist should I ask for?"
- "Does my question sound dumb?"
- "What if they transfer me?"

Online booking = **42% higher conversion for new visitors**.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### "But our clients are older ladies who don't book online..."

**Reality check**: 67% of 55+ uses smartphone for daily tasks. They book hotels, restaurants and even doctor's appointments online.

**Better argument**: Offer both options. Phone stays open, but online gives extra convenience.

### "Our schedule is too complex for online booking"

Modern systems can handle:
- Multi-stylist scheduling
- Service dependencies (color + cut)
- Variable slot lengths (half hour to 4 hours)
- Breaks and blocked times
- Staff vacations

**If you can plan it in your head, software can too.**

### "We want to screen clients first for complex colorings"

**Solution**: Consultation required feature. For new clients:
1. Client books free "Color Consultation" (15 min)
2. During consultation you determine treatment
3. Then book main appointment directly

Or: Add notes field where client can upload inspiration photos.
